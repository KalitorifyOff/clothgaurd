How to Use?

Step 1

Setup the "Cloth Guard System" terrace/ balcony/ anywhere respectively.

Step 2

Switch ON the system

Step 3

Open the rack & hang the clothes in the rack to dry.

Step 4

Select the Mode. If not specific select "Normal Mode"

Step 5

Connect to the Internet, So that the system can be controlled anywhere, if you are not available at the moment.

Step 6

Do't feel about your Cloths, Cloth Gaurd take care it.
